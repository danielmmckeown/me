# だい３か　デートのやくそく

# Lesson 3: Making a Date

## Dialogue 1: Mary and Takeshi are talking

**Listen to the dialogue and answer the questions:**

1. What does Mary usually do on the weekend?
2. Is Mary free on Saturday?
3. When are they going to see a movie?

### Japanese Text

**たけし：** メアリーさん、しゅうまつは たいてい なにを しますか。  
**めありい：** そうですね。たいてい うちで べんきょうします。でも、ときどき えいがを みます。  
**たけし：** そうですか…。じゃあ、どようびに えいがを みませんか。  
**めありい：** どようびは ちょっと…。  
**たけし：** じゃあ、にちようびは どうですか。  
**めありい：** いいですね。

### Romaji

**Takeshi:** Mearii-san, shuumatsu wa taitei nani wo shimasu ka.  
**Mary:** Sou desu ne. Taitei uchi de benkyou shimasu. Demo, tokidoki eiga wo mimasu.  
**Takeshi:** Sou desu ka... Jaa, doyoubi ni eiga wo mimasen ka.  
**Mary:** Doyoubi wa chotto...  
**Takeshi:** Jaa, nichiyoubi wa dou desu ka.  
**Mary:** Ii desu ne.

### English Translation

**Takeshi:** Mary-san, what do you usually do on weekends?  
**Mary:** Let's see... I usually study at home. But sometimes I watch movies.  
**Takeshi:** I see... Well then, would you like to see a movie on Saturday?  
**Mary:** Saturday is a little bit... (inconvenient)  
**Takeshi:** Well then, how about Sunday?  
**Mary:** That sounds good.

## Dialogue 2: On Sunday morning, at Mary's host family's

**Listen to the dialogue and answer the questions:**

1. Where will Mary go today?
2. What will she do there?
3. What time will she come home?
4. Will she eat dinner at home?

### Japanese Text

**めありい：** おはようございます。  
**おかあさん：** おはよう。はやい ですね。  
**めありい：** ええ、きょうは きょうとにいきます。きょうとでえいがをみます。  
**おかあさん：** いいですね。なんじごろ かえりますか。  
**めありい：** くじごろです。  
**おかあさん：** ばんごはんは？  
**めありい：** たべません。  
**おかあさん：** そうですか。じゃあ、いってらっしゃい。  
**めありい：** いってきます。

### Romaji

**Mary:** Ohayou gozaimasu.  
**Okaasan:** Ohayou. Hayai desu ne.  
**Mary:** Ee, kyou wa Kyouto ni ikimasu. Kyouto de eiga wo mimasu.  
**Okaasan:** Ii desu ne. Nanji goro kaerimasu ka.  
**Mary:** Kuji goro desu.  
**Okaasan:** Bangohan wa?  
**Mary:** Tabemasen.  
**Okaasan:** Sou desu ka. Jaa, itterasshai.  
**Mary:** Ittekimasu.

### English Translation

**Mary:** Good morning.  
**Host mother:** Good morning. You're up early, aren't you?  
**Mary:** Yes, today I'm going to Kyoto. I'm going to see a movie in Kyoto.  
**Host mother:** That's nice. Around what time will you come home?  
**Mary:** Around 9 o'clock.  
**Host mother:** What about dinner?  
**Mary:** I won't eat (at home).  
**Host mother:** I see. Well then, have a good trip!  
**Mary:** I'll go and come back!

---

## Answer Key

### Dialogue 1 Answers:

1. Mary usually studies at home, but sometimes watches movies.
2. No, Mary is not free on Saturday.
3. They're going to see a movie on Sunday.

### Dialogue 2 Answers:

1. Mary will go to Kyoto today.
2. She will see a movie there.
3. She will come home around 9 o'clock.
4. No, she will not eat dinner at home.
